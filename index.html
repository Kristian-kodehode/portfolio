About me

Hi, my name is Kristian and i am a frontend developer, graphic designer and UI/UX designer from Stavanger. I joined the frontend bootcamp with Kodehodene in March 2023 where i have been able to learn and grow my skills in HTML, CSS, Javascript, React and more . It has been a great experience and i am ready for the next step.

Beside being openminded, creative and loyal, i have a passion for creating, such as musicproduction, design, 3D and now, coding. I love tech and amongst those, AI has become a great asset in my workflow. Currently i am focusing on frontend skills like React, design and Typescript.

When I'm away from my laptop, I like playing with my cat Nora, helping my mom, playing Playstation, watching tv-shows and movies. I also try to get outside as much as possible, to enjoy the outdoors around where i live.
